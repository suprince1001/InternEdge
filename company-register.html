Hiring Policies

1. All internship and job opportunities must include clear role details, eligibility requirements, location, duration, and compensation information where applicable.

2. Companies must evaluate students fairly and must not discriminate based on gender, religion, caste, disability, nationality, or personal background.

3. Selection, rejection, interview, and onboarding communication should be professional, timely, and respectful.

4. Companies must not request unnecessary personal documents, money, deposits, training fees, or sensitive information from students.

5. Internship tasks must match the posted role and should not include unsafe, illegal, misleading, or unrelated work.

6. If a stipend, certificate, letter of recommendation, or pre-placement offer is promised, the company must honor the stated terms.

7. Student data shared through InternEdge must be used only for recruitment and internship-related communication.

8. Companies should maintain a safe work environment and follow all applicable labor, internship, and workplace conduct rules.

9. InternEdge may review, edit, reject, or remove opportunities that violate hiring standards or platform trust.

10. Repeated complaints, fraud, harassment, or policy violations may result in restricted access or permanent removal from the platform.
